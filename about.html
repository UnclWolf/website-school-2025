About Us

At Inglewood Sausage Co., we believe a great sausage is more than just food – it’s tradition, flavour, and community all wrapped into one. Proudly based in Inglewood, Taranaki, we craft small-batch sausages using locally sourced meats and the freshest ingredients. Each month we create a new, limited-edition flavour inspired by the seasons, classic Kiwi tastes, and bold international ideas. From smoky BBQ to spicy gourmet blends, our sausages are made to surprise your taste buds and bring something special to your table. Whether you’re firing up the BBQ, cooking a hearty family meal, or simply want to try something new, our Sausage of the Month subscription delivers quality, flavour, and excitement straight to your door. We’re passionate about keeping it local, supporting Taranaki producers, and sharing our love for sausages with food lovers across New Zealand. Because life’s too short for boring sausages.
